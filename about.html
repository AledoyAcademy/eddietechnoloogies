About us

Welcome

We hereby introduce our company “EDDIE TECHNOLOGIES NIGERIA LIMITED”. We are a leading Information Communication Technology company in Nigeria with our head office at 5, Godwin Omonuwa street, Off Ire Akari Estate, Isolo, Lagos, Nigeria. Established in the year 2007, Eddie Technologies continues to grow and expand by successfully proffering standardized and customized timely solutions/services to our client’s needs.

We can professionally Design, Configure and Implement ICT solutions as we have qualified and in house experts in all areas of information communication technology. Procurement of quality and durable ICT equipment, accessories and consumable also forms part of our strength as we are in collaboration with major international manufacturers.

Our Client Service is second to none. We provide effective and efficient service with focus on the right budget, right quality and on time service delivery. We can assure you our services/customized solutions combined will contribute immensely to the success and overall growth of your organization by proffering easier, effective and efficient means of job processes, marketing, service delivery and customer feedbacks.
